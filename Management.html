Senior management

Dr. Kabir M. Ashraf Alam ndc

Secretary General

Dr. Kabir M Ashraf Alam ndc, has joined Bangladesh Red Crescent Society (BDRCS) as the Secretary General on the 28 August 2024. He served as a member of Bangladesh Civil Service for more than 33 years. During this long tenure, he has traversed various fields of administration and management in the public sector.

Sultan Ahmed

Deputy Secretary General

Sultan Ahmed has joined Bangladesh Red Crescent Society (BDRCS) on 03 November 2022 as Deputy Secretary General. He is an accomplished management professional with 20 years of international experience in health and humanitarian program development and management, research and knowledge management, capacity building and organization development, and leading quality and accountability initiatives in the UN agencies and INGOs in Asia and Africa. He has worked with FAO, WFP, UNESCO, UNFPA, IFRC and British Red Cross.
